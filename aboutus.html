About TechHub

Your trusted partner for premium laptops, desktops, and accessories since 2020

Our Story

Welcome to TechHub, Sri Lanka's premier destination for cutting-edge technology solutions. Founded in 2020, we have been at the forefront of bringing the latest in computing technology to professionals, students, and tech enthusiasts across the island.

Located in the heart of Moratuwa, our state-of-the-art showroom and service center spans over 5,000 square feet, showcasing the latest laptops, desktop computers, and accessories from world-renowned brands. We pride ourselves on offering not just products, but complete technology solutions tailored to your specific needs.

Why Choose TechHub?

  • Expert technical support and consultation services
  • Competitive pricing with flexible payment options
  • Comprehensive warranty and after-sales service
  • Fast and reliable island-wide delivery
  • Professional setup and installation services
